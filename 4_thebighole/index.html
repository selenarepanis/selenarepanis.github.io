THE BIG HOLE

This is a project run by Izzy Hardisty and Selena Repanis, aiming to document "The Big Hole", formerly City Square. The Big Hole is currently being developed into what will be Town Hall Station in 2026. The Big Hole is situated in Narrm/Melbourne on stolen land.
